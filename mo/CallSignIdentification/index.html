mo:CallSignIdentification

element CallSignIdentification in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/militaryOperations/4.1/

Definition

An identification of any combination of characters or pronounceable words, which identifies a communication facility, a command, an authority, an activity, or a unit; used primarily for establishing and maintaining communications.
