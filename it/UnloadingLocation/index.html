it:UnloadingLocation

element UnloadingLocation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

Definition

An identification of a seaport, airport, freight terminal, rail station or other place at which goods are unloaded from the means of transport having been used for their carriage.
