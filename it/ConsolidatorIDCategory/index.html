it:ConsolidatorIDCategory

element ConsolidatorIDCategory in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

Definition

An identification of the freight forwarder combining individual smaller consignments into a single larger shipment (so called consolidated shipment) hat is sent to a counterpart who mirrors the consolidator's activity by dividing the consolidated consignment
