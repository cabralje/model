it:ImporterIDCategory

element ImporterIDCategory in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

Definition

A code for Identifier of party who makes - or on whose behalf a Customs clearing agent or other authorized person makes - an import declaration. This may include a person who has possession of the goods or to whom the goods are consigned.
