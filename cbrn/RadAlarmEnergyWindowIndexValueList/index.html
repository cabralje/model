cbrn:RadAlarmEnergyWindowIndexValueList

element RadAlarmEnergyWindowIndexValueList in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.1/

Definition

A list of one or more indices (If applicable) that indicate the position(s) of the value(s) in the WindowStartEnergyValues and WindowEndEnergyValues that triggered an alarm(s).
