cbrn:SiteSpecialInfoText

element SiteSpecialInfoText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.1/

Definition

A description of a site that may contain information of importance to a responder; e.g., electrified fence, dogs on property, loft apartment, multi-storied building, multiple warehouses on site, hazardous material, etc.
