scr:NCICDataIndicator

element NCICDataIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/screening/4.1/

Definition

True if the National Crime Information Center (NCIC) data administered by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) is either primary, fraudulent, or supplemental; false otherwise.
