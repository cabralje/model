im:AlienLookoutIndicator

element AlienLookoutIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.1/

Definition

True if the encounter is with an enrolled alien who may be an aggravated felon, may be a suspected narcotics smuggler, has an active arrest warrant, or may be a national security risk; false otherwise.
