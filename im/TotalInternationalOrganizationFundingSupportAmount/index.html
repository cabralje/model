im:TotalInternationalOrganizationFundingSupportAmount

element TotalInternationalOrganizationFundingSupportAmount in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.1/

Definition

A number indicating the funding amount provided by an International Organization to the Exchange Visitor (EV), in order to support his/her participation within a designated EV Program.
