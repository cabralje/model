biom:ImageCaptureDateEstimateRangeDuration

element ImageCaptureDateEstimateRangeDuration in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/4.1/

Definition

An optional field. It is entered in the format as YyyyyMmmDdd. It is possible to enter only a year, month and/or day range, such as P5D, meaning that the actual date of collection is estimated to be 5 days plus or minus that specified in Field 10.00
