ag:PlantedDateModifiedOtherReasonText

element PlantedDateModifiedOtherReasonText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

A free form text providing the reason that a planted date was modified from its original value when a reason other than the possible list of provided reasons is needed.
