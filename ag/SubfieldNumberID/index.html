ag:SubfieldNumberID

element SubfieldNumberID in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

An alpha number identifier that is used to uniquely identify a subfield within a field, within a tract within a farm within a state and county. Subfields are areas of a field that share a set of common planting characteristic in terms of crop, crop type, intended us
