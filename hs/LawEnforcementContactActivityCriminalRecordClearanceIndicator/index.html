hs:LawEnforcementContactActivityCriminalRecordClearanceIndicator

element LawEnforcementContactActivityCriminalRecordClearanceIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

True if the subject was cleared through the National Crime Information Center (NCIC) or a state, local, or tribal Crime Information Center at the time of contact; false otherwise.
