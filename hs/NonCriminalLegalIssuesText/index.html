hs:NonCriminalLegalIssuesText

element NonCriminalLegalIssuesText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A description of other cases and situations that may impact a court report. Tribal membership status and other ICWA issues. Includes prior juvenile (non-criminal) history, child support enforcement issues, mental health adjudications. Also identifies interstate placement issues (where there is an ICPC issue or UCCJEA issue). Also indicates the citizenship and legal residency status (if applicable).
