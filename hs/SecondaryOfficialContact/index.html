hs:SecondaryOfficialContact

element SecondaryOfficialContact in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A contact secondary to the primary state contact. It is to be used by states that have two departments who manage programs covered by PARIS.
