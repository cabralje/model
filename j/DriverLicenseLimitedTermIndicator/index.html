j:DriverLicenseLimitedTermIndicator

element DriverLicenseLimitedTermIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

True if a driver license or identification card is classified by DHS regulations as a temporary or limited-term document (i.e. the expiration date of the card is limited to the duration of an aliens legal stay in the U.S.); false otherwise.
