j:CaseInitiatingParty

element CaseInitiatingParty in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

An entity that brings charges or a suit against another in a court of law. Can be either a victim in a criminal case or a plaintiff in a civil case.
