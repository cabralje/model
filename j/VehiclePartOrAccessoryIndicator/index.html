j:VehiclePartOrAccessoryIndicator

element VehiclePartOrAccessoryIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

True if the part is a potential component or accessory for a motor-driven conveyance designed to carry its operator, and optionally passengers and cargo, over land; false otherwise.
